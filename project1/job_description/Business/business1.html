Reference Number: BA1234

Position Title: Business Analyst

Brief Description: Responsible for analyzing business processes, identifying areas for improvement, and helping companies optimize their operations to meet business objectives. Works closely with stakeholders to gather requirements and translate them into actionable solutions.

Salary Range: $70,000 - $100,000 per year

Reports To: Senior Business Analyst

Key Responsibilities
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to understand business needs and objectives
  • Analyze business processes and identify opportunities for improvement
  • Gather and document business requirements for new projects or system enhancements
  • Prepare detailed reports, presentations, and business cases to support decision-making
  • Translate business requirements into technical specifications for development teams
  • Facilitate meetings and workshops with stakeholders to ensure clear communication
  • Monitor project progress and provide regular updates to stakeholders
  • Ensure solutions align with organizational goals and strategies

Qualifications

Essential:

  • Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Information Systems, or a related field
  • Strong understanding of business processes and data analysis techniques
  • Experience with business requirements gathering, documentation, and process mapping
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint) and business analysis tools (e.g., Visio, JIRA, Confluence)
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • At least 3 years of experience as a Business Analyst

Preferable:

  • Experience with Agile methodologies and tools
  • Familiarity with data analytics, SQL, and database management
  • Certified Business Analysis Professional (CBAP) or other relevant certifications
  • Experience working in specific industries (e.g., finance, healthcare, technology)
