Reference Number: FA4567

Position Title: Financial Analyst

Brief Description: Responsible for analyzing financial data, preparing reports, and providing insights to support decision-making and business strategy. Works closely with finance teams and management to evaluate financial performance, identify trends, and recommend actions for improving profitability and efficiency.

Salary Range: $65,000 - $90,000 per year

Reports To: Finance Manager

Key Responsibilities
  • Prepare and analyze monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports
  • Monitor financial performance, identify trends, and provide actionable insights to management
  • Assist in the budgeting and forecasting process, ensuring alignment with business objectives
  • Analyze and interpret financial data to support investment and financial decisions
  • Evaluate financial risks and opportunities, and make recommendations for improvement
  • Conduct variance analysis to explain discrepancies between actual and projected financial results
  • Assist with financial modeling and scenario analysis to evaluate potential business strategies
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to optimize financial processes and controls

Qualifications

Essential:

  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or a related field
  • Strong understanding of financial principles, accounting, and reporting
  • Proficiency in financial software and tools (e.g., Excel, SAP, Oracle, QuickBooks)
  • Experience in financial analysis, budgeting, and forecasting
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and attention-to-detail skills
  • At least 3 years of experience in financial analysis or related roles

Preferable:

  • Certified Public Accountant (CPA) or Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) designation
  • Experience with financial modeling, forecasting tools, and data visualization software (e.g., Tableau, Power BI)
  • Familiarity with ERP systems and financial reporting systems
  • Strong communication skills and ability to present financial data to non-financial stakeholders
  • Experience in a specific industry (e.g., banking, insurance, corporate finance)
