Public Relations Specialist

Reference Number: ABC56

Position Title: Public Relations Specialist

Brief Description: Responsible for managing and enhancing the company’s public image, including developing media relations, crafting press releases, and executing PR campaigns that strengthen brand reputation and foster positive relationships with stakeholders.

Salary Range: $55,000 - $85,000 per year

Reports To: PR Manager

Key Responsibilities
  • Develop and implement public relations strategies that align with the company’s objectives and enhance its image
  • Build and maintain relationships with media outlets, journalists, and influencers
  • Create and distribute press releases, media kits, and other PR materials
  • Monitor media coverage and analyze PR campaign performance
  • Assist in the preparation and execution of events, press conferences, and interviews
  • Manage crisis communication and handle media inquiries during challenging situations
  • Collaborate with internal teams to ensure consistent brand messaging and communication
  • Provide insights and recommendations to improve public perception and media engagement

Qualifications

Essential:

  • Bachelor's degree in Public Relations, Communications, Journalism, or a related field
  • Proven experience in public relations, media relations, or communications
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong understanding of media landscape and PR best practices
  • Ability to craft compelling press releases and other PR materials
  • At least 2-3 years of experience in a public relations role

Preferable:

  • Experience working with digital media platforms and social media PR strategies
  • Knowledge of crisis communication and reputation management
  • Experience with media monitoring and analytics tools
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to think strategically
  • Ability to work under pressure and manage multiple tasks simultaneously
