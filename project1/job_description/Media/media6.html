Digital Marketing Specialist

Reference Number: ABC56

Position Title: Digital Marketing Specialist

Brief Description: Responsible for developing and executing digital marketing strategies to drive online visibility, engagement, and conversion. The Digital Marketing Specialist will manage campaigns across various digital channels, including social media, search engines, email marketing, and paid ads, to help achieve company growth goals.

Salary Range: $55,000 - $85,000 per year

Reports To: Marketing Manager

Key Responsibilities
  • Develop and execute comprehensive digital marketing campaigns across platforms, including Google Ads, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and email marketing
  • Monitor and analyze the performance of digital campaigns, making data-driven adjustments to improve effectiveness
  • Manage SEO/SEM strategies to increase website traffic and optimize content for search engines
  • Collaborate with the content team to create engaging and high-quality content for digital channels
  • Oversee and manage email marketing campaigns, including segmentation, targeting, and performance tracking
  • Develop and manage paid media campaigns, including budgeting, bidding strategies, and performance analysis
  • Conduct market research to identify opportunities and stay updated with digital marketing trends
  • Generate reports on key metrics, including ROI, website traffic, conversion rates, and engagement

Qualifications

Essential:

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field
  • Proven experience in digital marketing, including SEO, SEM, social media, and email marketing
  • Strong knowledge of digital marketing tools and platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager, Mailchimp)
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • At least 2-3 years of experience in digital marketing or a related role

Preferable:

  • Experience with content management systems (e.g., WordPress, Shopify)
  • Knowledge of marketing automation tools (e.g., HubSpot, Marketo)
  • Experience with conversion rate optimization and A/B testing
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and manage multiple campaigns simultaneously
  • Strong analytical skills and the ability to make data-driven decisions
