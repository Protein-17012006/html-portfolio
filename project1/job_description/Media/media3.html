Media Buyer

Reference Number: ABC56

Position Title: Media Buyer

Brief Description: Responsible for planning, negotiating, and purchasing media space to ensure that advertising campaigns reach the target audience effectively and efficiently across multiple platforms.

Salary Range: $55,000 - $85,000 per year

Reports To: Media Buying Manager

Key Responsibilities
  • Plan and execute media buying strategies for digital, TV, radio, print, and outdoor campaigns
  • Negotiate and purchase media space and time across various channels, ensuring maximum ROI
  • Monitor media campaigns and adjust as needed based on performance and analytics
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to align media strategies with overall marketing objectives
  • Maintain relationships with media vendors and ensure cost-effective buys
  • Track media spend and stay within budget while achieving campaign goals
  • Analyze market trends, audience insights, and competitor activity to inform media buying strategies
  • Generate and present reports on campaign performance to senior management

Qualifications

Essential:

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Advertising, or a related field
  • Proven experience in media buying, preferably in digital and traditional media
  • Strong negotiation and communication skills
  • Familiarity with media buying tools and platforms (e.g., Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager, DSPs)
  • Ability to analyze data and make strategic decisions based on performance metrics
  • At least 2-3 years of experience in media buying or a similar role

Preferable:

  • Experience with programmatic advertising and digital display networks
  • Familiarity with social media advertising platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter)
  • Strong knowledge of audience segmentation and targeting strategies
  • Experience with budgeting, forecasting, and reporting on media campaigns
  • Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment
