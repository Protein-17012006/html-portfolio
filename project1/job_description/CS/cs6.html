Automation Engineer

Reference Number: ABC56

Position Title: Automation Engineer

Brief Description: Responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining automation solutions to improve operational efficiency, reduce manual effort, and ensure quality across various systems and processes.

Salary Range: $80,000 - $110,000 per year

Reports To: Automation Engineering Manager

Key Responsibilities
  • Design, implement, and maintain automation solutions to streamline software development and operational processes
  • Write automated test scripts for functional and regression testing
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify areas for automation and process improvement
  • Develop and manage Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines
  • Monitor automated processes and ensure they run efficiently and accurately
  • Troubleshoot automation failures and make improvements to the systems
  • Ensure that automation processes meet business requirements and quality standards

Qualifications

Essential:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or Shell scripting
  • Experience with automation tools like Selenium, Jenkins, or Ansible
  • Strong understanding of CI/CD practices and pipeline management
  • Experience with version control systems like Git
  • At least 2-3 years of experience in an automation engineering role

Preferable:

  • Experience with cloud automation tools (e.g., AWS CloudFormation, Terraform)
  • Knowledge of containerization and orchestration tools like Docker and Kubernetes
  • Experience with performance testing and optimization tools
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Familiarity with Agile development practices
