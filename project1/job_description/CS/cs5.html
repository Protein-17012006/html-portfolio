Data Engineer

Reference Number: ABC56

Position Title: Data Engineer

Brief Description: Responsible for designing, building, and maintaining data systems and infrastructure, ensuring efficient collection, processing, and storage of data for analytics and machine learning models.

Salary Range: $90,000 - $120,000 per year

Reports To: Data Engineering Manager

Key Responsibilities
  • Design, build, and maintain scalable data pipelines to collect, process, and store large volumes of data
  • Work with cross-functional teams to define data requirements and ensure proper data integration
  • Develop and manage ETL processes (Extract, Transform, Load) to ensure data accuracy and quality
  • Optimize data storage solutions for scalability and performance (e.g., cloud data warehouses, big data tools)
  • Ensure data security and compliance with industry standards
  • Monitor data systems and troubleshoot issues as they arise
  • Collaborate with data scientists and analysts to prepare data for analysis and model training

Qualifications

Essential:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Data Engineering, or a related field
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, or Scala
  • Experience with relational and NoSQL databases (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Cassandra)
  • Strong knowledge of data warehousing and ETL concepts
  • Familiarity with big data technologies such as Hadoop, Spark, or Kafka
  • Experience with cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud
  • At least 2-3 years of experience in data engineering or a related role

Preferable:

  • Experience with data orchestration tools such as Apache Airflow
  • Knowledge of containerization and orchestration (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes)
  • Experience with data visualization tools like Tableau or Power BI
  • Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to optimize data systems for performance
  • Familiarity with machine learning frameworks or data science pipelines
