How to Win Friends and Influence People

How to Win Friends and Influence People by Dale Carnegie is a timeless self-help book that provides practical strategies for improving interpersonal relationships and communication. Through relatable anecdotes and actionable principles, it teaches readers how to make people like them, win others over to their way of thinking, and foster meaningful connections. Focused on empathy, active listening, and genuine appreciation, the book remains a cornerstone for personal and professional success.
